Wether monitoring and Crop Protection

This paper presents the development of Internet of Things application for crop protection to prevent animal intrusions in the crop field.A repelling and a monitoring system is provided to prevent potential damages in Agriculture, both from wild animal attacks and weather conditions. The proposed device will be monitoring the weather conditions like humidity, soil moisture, atmospheric temperature, etc.

Line following Robot

To construct an obstacle avoiding robot using ultrasonic sensor and Arduino.The circuit consists of Arduino UNO Board, HC-SR04 ultrasonic sensor, L293D Motor Driver IC, servo motor, a pair of DC Geared Motors of 200 RPM and two 3.7V Batteries.Using an external trigger signal, the Trig pin on ultrasonic sensor is made logic high for at least 10µs. A sonic burst from the transmitter module is sent. This consists of 8 pulses of 40KHz.

Voice controlled Robot

To construct a voice-controlled robot using Arduino and Bluetooth.The circuit consists of Arduino UNO Board, HC05/HC-06 Bluetooth Module, L293D Motor Driver IC, a pair of DC Geared Motors of 200 RPM and two 3.7V Batteries.Voice Commands are processed by phone, and speech-to-text conversion is done within the app using Google’s speech-recognition technology. The text is then sent to the receiver side via Bluetooth.
